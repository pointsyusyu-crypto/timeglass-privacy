Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Thank you for using TimeGlass ("the App"), developed by TimeGlass Developer ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Date of birth and target lifespan: Stored locally on your device to calculate your life countdown.
  • Bucket list items: Stored in the cloud via your Google Account (Firebase Firestore) to enable data backup and device transfer.
  • Google Account information: Your name and email address are collected when you sign in with Google, solely for the purpose of identifying your account and syncing your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate and display your life countdown
  • Save and sync your bucket list across devices
  • Identify your account for data management

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • Local data: Your date of birth, gender, and target lifespan are stored on your device only.
  • Cloud data: Your bucket list items are stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — for user authentication.

5. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App's settings. Upon deletion, all cloud-stored data will be permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
